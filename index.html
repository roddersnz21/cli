About Us

Making Neapolitan style pizza for over 20 years

Family owned pizzeria crazy about pizza. Baked in a woodfire oven, we bring excellent quality, delicious flavour and great service. That's how we conquered our loyal customers and became one of the top pizzerias in Ireland.

Fresh Ingredients

We offer the best Italian gastronomic excellence by using quality ingredients such as organically grown flour, vegetables and herbs. The integration between the ingredients and mixing allows us to create new proposals and update our menu.
